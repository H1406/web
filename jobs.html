Data Scientist

Reference Number:
DS987
Position Title:
Senior Data Scientist
Company:
Viettel Group
Salary Range:
$85,000 - $120,000 per year
Reports To:
Chief Data Officer

Company Overview:

Viettel Group is a leader in Vietnam’s telecommunications and technology sector, known for its innovation in digital services and commitment to building a connected, digital society. Viettel’s strategy revolves around using data and cutting-edge technology to deliver transformative solutions that meet the evolving needs of individuals and businesses globally.

Job Description:

We are seeking an experienced Data Scientist to join our data science team at Viettel Group. In this role, you will work on analyzing large datasets, developing machine learning models, and providing data-driven insights to support strategic business decisions across Viettel’s digital and telecommunications divisions.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Analyze large datasets to uncover trends and insights that support decision-making within Viettel's business operations.
  • Develop predictive models using machine learning algorithms to enhance service offerings and customer experiences.
  • Present data insights to stakeholders through detailed reports and presentations.
  • Work with software engineers and product teams to deploy machine learning models into production environments.

Required Qualifications:

Essential:

  1. Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Data Science, Computer Science, Statistics, or a related field.
  2. Experience with programming languages like Python or R.
  3. Experience with machine learning algorithms and frameworks (e.g., Scikit-Learn, TensorFlow).
  4. Proficiency in data manipulation using libraries such as Pandas and NumPy.
  5. Solid understanding of statistical methods and data mining techniques.

Preferable:

  • Experience working with large datasets and distributed computing tools (e.g., Hadoop, Spark).
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms such as AWS or Google Cloud.
  • Knowledge of data visualization tools (e.g., Matplotlib, Tableau).
  • Strong communication skills and the ability to translate technical data into actionable business insights.
